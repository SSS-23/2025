GYDA × Torch Group: Exploring the Future of Digital Finance and Youth Empowerment

Date

June 28, 2025

Location

Singapore

Partners

GYDA, Torch Group

About the Collaboration

GYDA has officially entered into collaboration with Torch Group, the official partner of the Asian Institute of Digital Finance (AIDF), a university-level institute at the National University of Singapore (NUS), jointly established by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), the National Research Foundation (NRF), and NUS.

This collaboration facilitates direct exchanges on topics at the intersection of artificial intelligence, digital finance, and sustainable innovation. Through Torch Group, GYDA gains access to expert conversations and institutional networks supporting frontier research and next-generation financial technology.

Focus Areas

The partnership focuses on two key areas:

  • Insight exchange: Sharing knowledge around responsible AI, financial inclusion, and innovation-driven policy development.
  • Youth engagement: Joint exploration of how to raise youth awareness, enhance early-stage talent development, and expand access to transformative technologies across sectors.

As part of GYDA’s broader Signal Catcher — Seminar initiative, this collaboration supports our mission to equip young people with the tools and perspectives needed for the digital and sustainable transitions ahead.
