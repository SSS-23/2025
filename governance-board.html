01 | Duty of the Governing Board

  1. Facilitate the Effective Organization of Council Meetings
    Ensures productive and efficient Council sessions through meticulous planning and coordination.
    • Develop Comprehensive Agendas: Craft detailed agendas that reflect the strategic priorities and diverse perspectives of member states, ensuring efficient and focused deliberations.
    • Manage Logistical Coordination: Oversee the logistical arrangements for Council meetings, including venue selection, interpretation services, and documentation translation, to ensure seamless participation across languages and time zones.
    • Ensure Timely Distribution of Meeting Materials: Prepare and distribute all relevant meeting materials, including reports, proposals, and background documents, well in advance to enable informed decision-making.
  2. Ensure the Effective Conveyance and Implementation of Council Resolutions
    Translates Council decisions into actionable directives and monitors their execution.
    • Clearly Communicate Council Directives: Translate Council resolutions into actionable directives for the executive body, ensuring clarity and precision in language and intent.
    • Establish Implementation Frameworks: Develop detailed implementation frameworks, including timelines, responsibilities, and resource allocations, to guide the executive body in executing Council decisions.
    • Monitor and Report on Progress: Implement a robust monitoring and reporting system to track the progress of resolution implementation, providing regular updates to the Council and member states.
  3. Serve as a Standing Council for Inter-sessional Decision-Making on Non-Major Issues
    Provides continuous guidance and makes necessary decisions between Council sessions.
    • Address Urgent Operational Matters: Respond promptly to urgent operational matters that arise between Council sessions, ensuring the continuity and efficiency of the organization's work.
    • Provide Guidance on Policy Interpretation: Offer guidance and clarification on the interpretation and application of existing policies and procedures, ensuring consistency and alignment across the organization.
    • Approve Minor Administrative and Financial Actions: Approve minor administrative and financial actions, such as budget adjustments and contract extensions, within established parameters, to facilitate smooth operations.
